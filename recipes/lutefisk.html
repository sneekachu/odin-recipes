Lutefisk

Odin's favorite lye-cured cod. Mmmm...gelatinous!

Ingredients

  • dried cod
  • lye

Steps

  1. Cure the cod in lye

    1. Add 2 tablespoons lye to a gallon of water.
    2. Soak cod in lye solution for 3 days.
    3. Soak cod in clear water for 4 days, changing the water every day.
    4. Soak the fish in water for 3 days.

  2. Cook the lutefisk

    1. Tie cod loosely in cheese cloth.
    2. Drop in large pot of boiling water.
    3. Boil for 10 minutes.
    4. Remove cheese cloth package and debone fish.
    5. Serve with butter and aquavit!
